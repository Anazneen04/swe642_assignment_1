About Our Department

Welcome to George Mason University's Department of Computer Science, where innovation meets education. Our department is at the forefront of technological advancement, offering cutting-edge programs that prepare students for leadership roles in the computing industry.

We pride ourselves on our diverse faculty, state-of-the-art research facilities, and strong industry connections. Our programs combine theoretical foundations with practical applications, ensuring our graduates are well-equipped for both industry positions and academic pursuits.

Our Master's Programs

  • MS in Computer Science

    Advanced study in algorithms, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.

  • MS in Information Security

    Specialized focus on cybersecurity, network security, and secure software development.

  • MS in Information Systems

    Comprehensive study of database systems, information architecture, and system design.

  • MS in Data Analytics Engineering

    In-depth exploration of data mining, big data analytics, and predictive modeling.
